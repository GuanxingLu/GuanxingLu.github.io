Research

My research passion is on "building general agents for open environments". I am concerned with how to discover primitives in decision series or world models that contain common sense of an open environment, and how to combine them to solve creative tasks efficiently. Specifically, I am interested in Vision-Language Learning, Video Understanding and Data-Efficient Learning.

I have some research experiences in computer vision and machine learning. Most of my previous work focuses on how to leverage the proper prior knowledge to boost performance in particular applications.
